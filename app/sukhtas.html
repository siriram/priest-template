Sukhtas

  • Akandamandalaakaaram vyaaptam yena charaacharam; Tatpadam darshitam yena tasmai shree Gurave namah.

    (My) salutations to that Guru who showed me the abode, the one who is known, whose form is the entire universe and by whom all that moves and that does not move is pervaded.
